About Southern Barn Builders

A family-owned builder rooted in the South, raising barns that stand up to whatever the Gulf and the Georgia pines can throw at them.

Our Story

Built On Handshakes & Hard Work

Southern Barn Builders LLC started where most good Southern businesses do -- in a pickup truck, with a tape measure, and a handshake deal to raise a neighbor's barn. What began as a passion project has grown into a full-service pole barn company serving every corner of Florida and reaching up into South Georgia.

We're not a franchise. We're not a national kit dealer. We're your neighbors -- the folks you see at the feed store, at the county fair, and at the boat ramp on Saturday mornings. When we put our name on a barn, it means something.

Our Values

What We Stand For

1

Honest Pricing

You get a clear, itemized quote. No surprise charges, no bait-and-switch upgrades, no "oh we forgot to mention" line items halfway through the build.

2

Quality Materials

Pressure-treated posts rated for ground contact, full-length lumber, 29-gauge (or heavier) metal roofing, and hardware that won't rust out after one Florida summer.

3

Craftsman Crews

Our builders have decades of combined experience raising barns in the South. Not seasonal help -- career framers who take pride in a straight line and a plumb post.

4

Local Accountability

You deal with the same point of contact from the first site visit through the final walk-through. No call-center runaround, no ghosting after the deposit clears.

5

Engineered For The South

Every barn is designed for the wind, rain, heat, and humidity that define Florida and Georgia. We build for where we live.

6

Standing Behind The Work

Our builds are backed by written warranties on materials and workmanship. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Credentials

Licensed. Insured. Accountable.

Southern Barn Builders LLC is a fully licensed and insured contractor in the State of Florida and operates under reciprocal licensing and permit partnerships in Georgia. Every build is permitted, inspected, and engineered to code.
