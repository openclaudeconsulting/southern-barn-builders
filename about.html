About Perez Construction

A family-run general contractor serving Florida and South Georgia with roofing, pole barns, fencing, porches, decks, and the finish work that makes a project feel complete.

Our Story

Built On Handshakes & Hard Work

Perez Construction LLC is a family-run contracting business serving homeowners, ranchers, and commercial clients across Florida and into South Georgia. We started taking on jobs the way most good contractors do — one handshake at a time, with a truck, a tape measure, and a reputation to protect.

We're not a franchise and we're not a call-center brand. We're a working crew — the people who actually show up, set the posts, hang the drip edge, and walk the finished job with you. When we put our name on a project, it means something.

Our Values

Honest Pricing
