Commercial & Residential

What We Build

Barns, barndominiums, stables, workshops, garages — and anything else you can build on a pole-barn frame. G&P Steel Trusses handles it from drawings to walk-through.

1

Barns

Agricultural, livestock, and general-purpose barns — open-sided, enclosed, or loafing. Sized to your herd, equipment, and workflow, and engineered for Florida wind loads and Georgia storms.

2

Barndominiums

Living space and working space under one roof. Pole-barn shell with a finished interior — bedrooms, kitchen, shop bay, however you want to live in it. Built to residential code from the ground up.

3

Stables

Horse stables with clean center aisles, proper ventilation, tack and feed rooms, and stall layouts that make daily chores easier. Dutch doors, wash racks, and run-outs available.

4

Workshops

Wide-open spans for equipment, tools, and elbow room. Insulation, electrical rough-in, roll-up doors, and concrete floors finished to the level your trade actually needs.

5

Garages

Single, double, and oversized garages for vehicles, RVs, boats, and toys. Detached or matched to your home. Doors sized to what you actually need to drive in.

6

Custom Pole-Barn Builds

Storage buildings, covered arenas, event space, commercial shells — if it can be built on a pole-barn frame, we can fabricate, supply, and install it. Tell us what you're after.
