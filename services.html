Commercial & Residential

What We Build

From a new roof over your head to a custom pole barn, fence, porch, or deck — Perez Construction handles the full project, top to bottom.

1

Roofing

Full roof replacements, new construction, and repairs for residential homes and commercial buildings. Metal standing-seam, shingle, and specialty systems — with code-compliant underlayment, flashing, and ventilation done right the first time.

2

Pole Barn Installation

Custom pole barns for agricultural, equestrian, workshop, storage, and residential use. Open-sided, enclosed, barndominium, or workshop layouts — engineered for Florida wind loads and Georgia storms.

3

Fencing

Wood privacy, vinyl, chain-link, ranch-rail, and custom fencing. Property-line marked, gates hung true, posts set to depth. Commercial perimeter and residential backyard work alike.

4

Porches

Covered porches, screened enclosures, wrap-arounds, and front-porch additions. Tied into your existing roofline or freestanding, with matching materials and finishes.

5

Decks

Pressure-treated, composite, and hardwood decks for backyards, pool surrounds, and multi-level layouts. Railings, stairs, and built-in features all in-house.

6

Steps & Stairs

Concrete steps, wooden stair systems, handrails, and code-compliant exterior stairways. Safe, square, and built to last — the finish work that ties the whole project together.

7

Garages & Workshops

Detached garages, hobby shops, and mechanic-grade workspaces. Roll-up doors, insulated walls, electrical rough-in, and concrete floors available.

8

Commercial Projects

Warehouses, storage facilities, contractor yards, and light commercial buildings throughout Florida and Georgia. Permit-ready plans and timeline accountability.

9

Repairs & Retrofits

Storm damage repair, re-roofing, post replacement, deck resurfacing, and fence rebuilds. We fix what other crews walked away from — call before you tear it down.
