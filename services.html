9 Building Types

What We Build

From single-stall horse barns to 10,000 sq ft commercial storage facilities -- if it's got posts in the ground and a roof over the top, we build it right.

1

Agricultural Pole Barns

Hay barns, equipment sheds, livestock shelters, and loafing sheds built for working ag operations. Open-sided or fully enclosed, with or without lean-tos. The backbone of every Southern farm.

2

Equestrian Barns

Shedrow, center-aisle, and breezeway horse barns with custom stalls, tack and feed rooms, wash bays, and hay lofts. Built for the Florida climate and Georgia hill country alike.

3

Garages & Workshops

Detached garages, hobby shops, and mechanic-grade workspaces. Roll-up doors, insulated walls, electrical rough-in, and concrete floors all available.

4

RV & Boat Covers

Tall-clearance covers sized for Class A motorhomes, fifth wheels, offshore boats, and tandem trailers. Single-bay or multi-bay configurations.

5

Commercial & Storage

Warehouses, mini-storage facilities, contractor yards, and light commercial buildings throughout Florida and Georgia. Permit-ready plans available.

6

Barndominiums

Full pole-barn-style homes and live-work combos. Open floor plans, cathedral ceilings, metal exteriors, and the modern country look buyers are after. The fastest-growing segment we build.

7

Riding Arenas & Covered Arenas

Clear-span covered arenas for trainers, ranchers, and private owners. Proper ventilation, kick walls, and footing prep included in every design.

8

Pavilions & Event Barns

Wedding venues, outdoor kitchens, picnic pavilions, and family gathering structures. Timber accents and cupolas available.

9

Re-Roofs & Barn Repairs

Metal re-roofing, post replacement, storm damage repair, and retrofit upgrades on existing barns. We fix what other crews walked away from. If your barn has seen better days, call us before you tear it down.
